About Us

  • History

    SVI Limited was established in 1998 to provide high quality IT services to businesses in the local area. The services that were provided ranged from desktop support to the commissioning of bespoke software solutions. During 1999 SVI was selected to project manage the design, specification and installation of a new ERP system for a major north sea oil services company. The development and subsequent support of the ERP system, opened several new doors to us and also gave rise to opportunities to expand our client base into many different countries and various industries.

  • Clients

    Although we are based in Scotland we have several multinational clients that have bases in countries in every major continent. We provide product support and development to many of these disparate locations. Our client base comes from a range of industries, from automotive to energy services, and from oil and gas services to telecommunications, and we provide a service specifically tailored to each client's requirements.

  • Integration

    We have experience of integrating custom solutions into several enterprise environments. The customer systems that we have integrated our solutions into range from payroll, and finance systems, to inventory management and CAD systems with several systems in between.
